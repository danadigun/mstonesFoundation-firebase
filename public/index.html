Milestones Rehabilitation Foundation was founded in 2011, to promote the development and dissemination of evidence based prevention and treatment services for Substance Use Related (HIV & Hepatitis) and Addictive Disorders in Nigeria. Since its inception, the non-profit Foundation has organized and supported programs aimed at both Improving Service Delivery for People who Use Drugs, and Advocacy.

From the Director of programs - Joseph O. Ike

The Foundation designs and executes Programs that cut across a number of Key Thematic Areas:

  • Prevention & Treatment of Substance Use Disorders
  • Harm Reduction Strategies and Interventions for People who Use Drugs
  • Interventions for Special Populations, specifically
    • Female Drug Users
    • Street Children & Juvenile Delinquents
    • Internally Displaced People.


For each of these areas Milestones Programs focus on:

  • Training & Capacity Building Interventions for Service Providers, to promote access to evidence based services and interventions,
  • Research to provide evidence basis for our specific context both to improve services and advocate for more effective policies, and
  • Advocacy. Our advocacy agenda is determined by thorough evaluation of our context, and promoting effective policies that decrease the incidence of Problematic Substance Use, and the impact of Substance Use while promoting the rights of people who use drugs and a healthy functional community.

And we take into consideration:

  • Working with members of the affected communities in design and implementation of programs targeted at them,
  • Adhering to strict ethical standards, in the implementation of our programs,
  • Keeping our Processes open and transparent, while respecting and protecting the confidentiality of the people we serve.

While we do appreciate the financial support of our donors over the year, we have the greatest respect and gratitude for the volunteers that have provided their time and expertise to promote the work we do, and welcome all who are interested to contact us for ways that we could work together.
